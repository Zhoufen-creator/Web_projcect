TONG HOP CAC CHUC NANG HIEN CO CUA WEBSITE

1. CHUC NANG CHUNG
- Dang nhap bang email va mat khau.
- Dang ky tai khoan cong khai tren he thong.
- Tai khoan dang ky moi se duoc tao voi vai tro Patient.
- Sau khi dang ky thanh cong, he thong tu tao ho so benh nhan.
- Sau khi dang ky thanh cong, he thong tu dong dang nhap va chuyen vao khu vuc Patient.
- Tu dong dieu huong den khu vuc dung voi vai tro sau khi dang nhap:
  Admin, Doctor, Patient, Employee.
- Dang xuat khoi he thong.
- Chan truy cap trai quyen qua trang AccessDenied.
- Co trang Home, Privacy va trang loi Error.
- Tu dong tao san cac role he thong:
  Admin, Doctor, Employee, Patient.
- Tu dong seed cac tai khoan mau:
  admin@gmail.com
  doctor@gmail.com
  employee@gmail.com
  patient@gmail.com

2. CHUC NANG CHO BENH NHAN
- Xem dashboard ca nhan.
- Xem thong tin benh nhan co ban.
- Xem lich hen sap toi.
- Xem tong so lich hen da dat.
- Xem tong so lan kham.
- Cap nhat ho so ca nhan:
  ho ten, email, so dien thoai, ngay sinh, gioi tinh, dia chi.
- Cap nhat thong tin y te:
  nhom mau, chieu cao, can nang, so BHYT, tien su benh, di ung.
- Xem danh sach lich hen cua minh.
- Dat lich kham moi.
- Nhap ly do kham khi dat lich.
- He thong du doan chuyen khoa tu trieu chung/ly do kham.
- He thong goi y bac si phu hop theo chuyen khoa va khung gio.
- Tu dong gan bac si neu tim duoc bac si phu hop.
- Kiem tra tinh hop le cua lich hen:
  bac si ton tai, co ca truc, khong trung gio, ca truc chua vuot qua so benh nhan toi da.
- Tao lich hen voi trang thai Pending.
- Tao thong bao khi dat lich thanh cong.
- Gui email xac nhan dat lich.
- Huy lich hen.
- Nhap ly do huy lich.
- Khong cho huy neu lich da check-in.
- Khong cho huy neu lich da hoan thanh.
- Khong cho huy neu lich da huy truoc do.
- Tao thong bao khi huy lich thanh cong.
- Gui email xac nhan huy lich.
- Chuc nang chong spam dat lich:
  neu dat qua 5 lich trong 1 ngay thi tai khoan bi danh dau spam.
- Tao thong bao canh bao khi tai khoan bi danh dau spam.
- Chan dat lich tiep neu tai khoan dang bi danh dau spam.
- Xem lich su kham benh.
- Xem thong tin phieu kham:
  ngay kham, bac si, chuyen khoa, trieu chung, chan doan, dan do.
- Xem don thuoc da ke.
- Xem cac dich vu y te da thuc hien.
- Xem danh sach thong bao ca nhan.
- Tu dong danh dau thong bao da doc khi mo trang thong bao.

3. CHUC NANG CHO BAC SI
- Xem dashboard bac si.
- Xem lich hen trong ngay hom nay.
- Xem tong so benh nhan da tiep nhan.
- Xem tong so phieu kham da lap.
- Xem toan bo lich hen cua minh.
- Xem rieng lich hen hom nay.
- Cap nhat ho so bac si:
  ho ten, ngay sinh, gioi tinh, dia chi, so dien thoai.
- Cap nhat thong tin nghe nghiep:
  chuyen khoa, so giay phep, bang cap/chung chi.
- Tao phieu kham tu lich hen.
- Chinh sua phieu kham da ton tai.
- Ghi nhan trieu chung.
- Ghi nhan chan doan.
- Ghi nhan loi dan/do can tranh.
- Cap nhat trang thai kham.
- Ke thuoc cho benh nhan.
- Ghi nhan dich vu y te da thuc hien.
- Xem lich su kham cua benh nhan do chinh bac si do phu trach.

4. CHUC NANG CHO NHAN VIEN
- Xem dashboard nhan vien.
- Xem toan bo danh sach lich hen.
- Chinh sua lich hen:
  doi bac si, doi gio kham, doi trang thai.
- Kiem tra hop le lich hen truoc khi cap nhat.
- Tao thong bao cho benh nhan khi lich hen duoc cap nhat.
- Gui email thong bao khi lich hen duoc cap nhat.
- Check-in benh nhan.
- Tu dong chuyen trang thai tu Pending sang Confirmed khi check-in.
- Tao thong bao cho benh nhan khi check-in thanh cong.
- Gui email khi check-in thanh cong.
- Quan ly ca truc bac si:
  xem danh sach, them, sua, xoa.
- Kiem tra gio ket thuc phai lon hon gio bat dau khi tao/sua ca truc.
- Xem danh sach thong bao.
- Gui thong bao thu cong cho benh nhan.

5. CHUC NANG CHO QUAN TRI VIEN
- Xem dashboard quan tri.
- Xem tong so nguoi dung.
- Xem tong so bac si.
- Xem tong so benh nhan.
- Xem tong so lich hen.
- Xem thong ke lich hen theo trang thai:
  Pending, Completed, Cancelled.
- Xem danh sach lich hen gan day.
- Xem phan tich tai chuyen khoa.
- Quan ly nguoi dung:
  xem danh sach, tao moi, chinh sua, xoa.
- Gan vai tro cho tai khoan khi tao moi.
- Tai khoan Doctor va Employee duoc tao/phan quyen boi Admin, khong dang ky cong khai.
- Doi vai tro cua tai khoan khi chinh sua.
- Kiem tra email trung khi tao/sua tai khoan.
- Khong cho xoa tai khoan admin goc admin@gmail.com.
- Khi xoa tai khoan se xoa/thu hoi du lieu lien quan:
  ho so patient, doctor, employee, lich hen, phieu kham, don thuoc, thong bao, lich su email, luot truy cap.

6. CHUC NANG HE THONG CHAY NEN VA HO TRO NGHIEP VU
- Gui email qua SMTP.
- Luu lich su gui email vao he thong.
- Tu dong nhac lich kham theo nen.
- Chu ky xu ly nhac lich la moi 5 phut.
- Gui nhac lich truoc 24 gio.
- Gui nhac lich truoc 2 gio.
- Tao thong bao khi den moc nhac lich.
- Gui email nhac lich cho benh nhan.
- Danh dau da gui nhac 24h de tranh gui lap.
- Danh dau da gui nhac 2h de tranh gui lap.
- Uoc luong thoi gian cho kham cho lich hen.
- Tinh so benh nhan phia truoc.
- Tinh thoi gian kham trung binh cua bac si.
- Dua ra goi y gio den.
- Phan tich tai theo chuyen khoa cho admin.
- Phat hien chuyen khoa co dau hieu tang ca bat thuong.
- Dua ra khuyen nghi tang bac si truc hoac mo rong ca kham.

TONG HOP CAC CHUC NANG NANG CAO CUA HE THONG
GHI CHU DE HIEU CHO SINH VIEN LAM VIEC NHOM

Muc dich cua file nay:
- Khong liet ke cac chuc nang CRUD thong thuong nhu them, sua, xoa.
- Chi tap trung vao cac chuc nang duoc xem la nang cao hon trong he thong.
- Moi muc se co:
  chuc nang, mo hinh/cach tiep can, thuat toan/cach xu ly, thu vien/cong nghe, ghi chu de hieu.

==================================================================
1. XAC THUC, PHAN QUYEN VA DIEU HUONG THEO VAI TRO
==================================================================

Chuc nang:
- Dang nhap bang email va mat khau.
- Dang xuat khoi he thong.
- Chan truy cap trai quyen.
- Tu dong chuyen nguoi dung vao dung khu vuc:
  Admin, Doctor, Patient, Employee.

Mo hinh:
- RBAC (Role-Based Access Control) = phan quyen theo vai tro.

Thuat toan/cach xu ly:
- Nguoi dung nhap email + mat khau.
- He thong tim user theo email.
- Neu dung mat khau thi kiem tra user dang thuoc role nao.
- Role nao thi redirect vao dashboard cua role do.

Thu vien/cong nghe:
- ASP.NET Core Identity
- Authorize Attribute
- Cookie Authentication

Ghi chu de hieu:
- Day la phan "bao ve he thong".
- Nhom co the trinh bay rang: "He thong phan quyen theo vai tro, moi vai tro co khu vuc rieng."
- Day la mot chuc nang nang cao hon dang nhap don thuan vi no co phan quyen va dieu huong role.

==================================================================
2. DANG KY CONG KHAI CHI CHO BENH NHAN
==================================================================

Chuc nang:
- Nguoi dung moi co the tu dang ky tai khoan.
- Tai khoan dang ky moi chi duoc tao voi vai tro Patient.
- Sau khi dang ky, he thong tu tao ho so Patient.
- Sau khi dang ky thanh cong, he thong tu dong dang nhap.

Mo hinh:
- Public Registration + Role Assignment.

Thuat toan/cach xu ly:
- Kiem tra email da ton tai hay chua.
- Tao ApplicationUser.
- Gan role Patient.
- Tao ban ghi Patient lien ket voi UserId.
- Dang nhap ngay sau khi tao thanh cong.

Thu vien/cong nghe:
- ASP.NET Core Identity
- Entity Framework Core

Ghi chu de hieu:
- Diem quan trong o day la:
  benh nhan duoc dang ky cong khai.
- Con Doctor va Employee khong duoc dang ky cong khai, ma do Admin tao.
- Khi thuyet trinh, co the noi:
  "He thong kiem soat duoc nguoi nao duoc tu tao tai khoan, nguoi nao phai do quan tri tao."

3. DU DOAN CHUYEN KHOA TU TRIEU CHUNG
==================================================================

Chuc nang:
- Khi benh nhan nhap ly do kham, he thong goi y chuyen khoa phu hop.

Mo hinh:
- Rule-based classification.
- Nghia la khong dung AI hoc may phuc tap, ma dua tren tap luat va tu khoa.

Thuat toan/cach xu ly:
- Chuan hoa chuoi nhap vao.
- Bo dau tieng Viet.
- So khop voi danh sach tu khoa cua tung chuyen khoa.
- Dem so lan khop.
- Chuyen khoa nao co diem cao nhat thi duoc chon.
- Neu khong co tu khoa nao khop thi fallback ve "Noi tong quat".

Thu vien/cong nghe:
- C# thuần
- Dictionary
- StringBuilder

Ghi chu de hieu:
- Day la mot dang "he thong goi y theo luat".
- Khong phai AI machine learning, nhung van la mot tinh nang thong minh.
- Neu giang vien hoi "thuat toan la gi", co the tra loi:
  "Thuat toan so khop tu khoa va tinh diem theo chuyen khoa."

==================================================================
4. GOI Y BAC SI TU DONG THEO CHUYEN KHOA VA TAI
==================================================================

Chuc nang:
- Sau khi du doan chuyen khoa, he thong tiep tuc goi y bac si phu hop.

Mo hinh:
- Recommendation System don gian.
- Load Balancing trong pham vi bac si.

Thuat toan/cach xu ly:
- Loc cac bac si dung chuyen khoa.
- Kiem tra bac si co ca truc trong khung gio da chon hay khong.
- Kiem tra ca do con cho trong hay da du benh nhan.
- Kiem tra co trung lich hay khong.
- Chon bac si co it benh nhan hon.
- Neu bang nhau thi uu tien bac si con nhieu slot hon.

Thu vien/cong nghe:
- Entity Framework Core
- LINQ

Ghi chu de hieu:
- Day la diem rat hay de trinh bay vi no cho thay he thong "khong chi luu du lieu" ma con "ho tro ra quyet dinh".
- Co the noi ngan gon:
  "He thong tu phan bo de tranh qua tai mot bac si."

10. PHAN TICH TAI CHUYEN KHOA
==================================================================

- Anomaly detection don gian.

Thuat toan/cach xu ly:
- Lay so lieu lich hen cua 12 tuan truoc.
- Tinh trung binh theo tung chuyen khoa.
- Tinh so luong cua tuan hien tai.
- So sanh current week voi average.
- Neu tang >= 1.5 lan thi xem la bat thuong.
- Sinh recommendation tu dong.

Thu vien/cong nghe:
- Entity Framework Core
- LINQ

Ghi chu de hieu:
- Day la tinh nang co tinh chat "phan tich du lieu".
